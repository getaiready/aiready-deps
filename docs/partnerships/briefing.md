- **Target Audience:** CTOs, Engineering Managers, and Tech-forward agencies.
- **Status:** Hybrid Open Source and Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS).
- **The Opportunity:**
  - **Lead Generation:** Use the "AI-Readiness Audit" as a high-value lead magnet for engineering teams.
  - **Community Growth:** Building a network of early adopters who want to lead the AI-agent revolution.
- **Value Prop:** Reduces "AI Tax" and allows teams to adopt AI coding agents 10x faster.
- **Website:** [getaiready.dev](https://getaiready.dev)

## 3. ClawMore (Innovation & Managed Evolution)

_The Future: Evolution-as-a-Service (EaaS)._

- **Target Audience:** Startups and innovation teams wanting to move from "Static Code" to "Living Repositories."
- **Status:** Infant stage / Rapid updates (Full launch expected in ~2 weeks).
- **The Opportunity:**
  - **Creative Growth:** Piloting autonomous codebase maintenance with "Co-evolution Partners."
  - **Managed AI Infrastructure:** A unique service offering for Radjal’s agency to bundle for high-end clients.
- **Core Concept:** $29/mo for a managed, secure, and self-healing AWS environment that evolves with your business.
- **Vision:** [clawmore.ai](https://clawmore.ai)
